What is SERB?

We all know that making a resume is time consuming and frustrating. It can be hard to fit all of your valuable experience onto one page. Some websites offer templates to get you started but often times they leave your resume looking unprofessional and messy. Fix that with the Space Efficient Resume Builder. With SERB you can quickly and easily organize all of your experience onto one page in a clean and efficient manner in a matter of minutes. What makes us better than all the other companies? Utilizing machine learning by building a convolutional neural network trained on already successful resume layouts, our algorithm will consistently produce the best possible resume for you. You can use the app for free or upgrade to a premium account for $1.99 a month to access more templates and print more resumes at once. As well as negotiable partnerships with Universities.
